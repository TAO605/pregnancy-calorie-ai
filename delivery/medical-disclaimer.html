Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: 05/13/2026

Important medical disclaimer: this tool is for nutrition reference only. It does not replace diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, or personalized care from a doctor, midwife, registered dietitian, or other qualified healthcare professional.

Not a substitute for professional care

The calorie estimates, macro targets, pregnancy weight gain references, meal ideas, AI-generated nutrition summaries, and movement suggestions are educational references. They are not a replacement for advice from an obstetrician, physician, midwife, registered dietitian, or qualified healthcare professional who knows your personal health history.

How to use the calculator safely

  • Use the results as a discussion starting point, not as a final instruction.
  • Review personal calorie goals, weight changes, appetite issues, supplements, and activity plans with your care team.
  • Follow your clinician's guidance if you have gestational diabetes, hypertension, multiple pregnancy, eating-disorder history, fetal growth concerns, or other pregnancy complications.

Guideline-based educational estimates

The tool uses educational nutrition concepts, including Mifflin-St Jeor resting energy estimation, activity factors, pregnancy calorie additions, macro ranges, and Institute of Medicine / National Academies pregnancy weight gain references. These references are general and may not fit every person.

AI guidance limitations

AI-generated text can make nutrition information easier to understand, but it may not know your full medical history, lab results, symptoms, allergies, medications, or clinician instructions. The site constrains AI responses to nutrition education and avoids diagnosis or treatment advice.

Review process and source support

AI-generated nutrition explanations are checked against site safety rules before display, including nutrition-only scope, no diagnosis, no treatment claims, no medication instructions, and clear care-team reminders. Public medical and nutrition statements are reviewed against source materials from the World Health Organization (WHO) and American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG).

The site content team has product and research experience in AI-powered pregnancy nutrition education, but this review is not a substitute for review by your own obstetrician, physician, registered dietitian, midwife, or qualified healthcare professional.

When to seek help

Contact your healthcare provider for symptoms, fetal movement concerns, rapid weight changes, severe nausea, dehydration, blood pressure concerns, blood sugar concerns, or any situation that feels worrying. For urgent symptoms, call your local emergency number.

Contact for corrections

If you notice unclear or outdated educational content, contact support@aipregnancycaloriecalculator.online.
