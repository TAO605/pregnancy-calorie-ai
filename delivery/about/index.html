About Our AI Pregnancy Calorie Calculator

Last updated: 05/13/2026

AI Pregnancy Calorie Calculator is an English SaaS tool that helps pregnant users estimate daily calorie goals, macro targets, meal planning ideas, weekly weight trends, and AI pregnancy nutrition summaries. It is built for education, not diagnosis or treatment.

Our mission

Pregnancy nutrition numbers can feel confusing. Our goal is to turn core inputs such as pregnancy week, pre-pregnancy weight, height, activity level, and pregnancy type into plain-English guidance that users can discuss with their own healthcare provider.

What the calculator does

  • Estimates daily pregnancy calorie needs using a transparent calorie formula and pregnancy-week adjustment.
  • Shows macro targets for protein, carbohydrates, and fats with practical food examples.
  • Provides trimester and week-aware nutrition summaries, meal planning examples, and weight-trend references.
  • Uses AI to make educational guidance easier to read and more relevant to the user's inputs.

Method and references

The tool is designed around widely used nutrition principles, including Mifflin-St Jeor resting energy estimation, activity factors, pregnancy calorie additions, and the Institute of Medicine / National Academies pregnancy weight gain guidance. The site also references mainstream public-health guidance from the World Health Organization (WHO) and American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG).

Editorial and AI review approach

AI output is constrained to pregnancy nutrition education, calorie planning, meal ideas, and gentle movement references. The website avoids diagnosis, medication advice, treatment claims, and emergency guidance. Content is reviewed and adjusted to stay plain, supportive, and aligned with the site's medical disclaimer.

Who operates the site

The website is operated as a personal independent SaaS project by Tao Xu. For support, corrections, privacy requests, billing questions, or content feedback, contact support@aipregnancycaloriecalculator.online.

How We Developed Our Tool

The project was developed by studying pregnancy calorie estimation methods, public pregnancy nutrition guidance, and common user questions around trimester calorie needs, macro targets, meal planning, and weekly weight tracking. The calculator is tested with multiple pregnancy-week, BMI, activity, and pregnancy-type scenarios before updates are deployed.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

We keep the calculation process transparent, show the formula steps on the result page, and separate educational nutrition guidance from medical decisions. AI-generated content is reviewed through prompt boundaries and content checks so it stays focused on pregnancy nutrition support.

Important medical boundary

This website is not a healthcare provider. Users should use the results as educational starting points and review personal pregnancy nutrition, weight gain, symptoms, or medical conditions with an obstetrician, physician, registered dietitian, midwife, or qualified healthcare professional.
