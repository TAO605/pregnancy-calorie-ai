Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how AI Pregnancy Calorie Calculator uses essential cookies, local storage, and similar browser technologies to run the calculator, account login, AI cache, and subscription access.

Cookies and local storage

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. Local storage is a browser feature that keeps information on your device. We use these technologies only where they support site functionality, security, or convenience.

How this site uses browser storage

  • Calculator memory: local storage may remember your age, height, weight, pregnancy week, activity level, unit setting, and optional preferences.
  • Weekly features: local storage may save pregnancy week, save date, weekly weight records, and report preferences so the tracker can work on return visits.
  • AI response cache: repeated AI guidance requests for the same input set may load faster through local caching.
  • Account session: secure session cookies support login, logout, password reset state, and Premium access checks.
  • Subscription processing: Stripe may use its own cookies or browser technologies during checkout and customer portal sessions.

Analytics and advertising cookies

The current website does not rely on optional advertising cookies. Hosting providers may still process technical logs to deliver the website, prevent abuse, diagnose errors, and measure reliability.

How to manage storage

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Clearing site data may remove saved calculator inputs, weekly records, cached AI guidance, and login state.
