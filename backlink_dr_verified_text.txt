Domain authority plays a major role in search engine visibility. One of the most widely used metrics to evaluate website strength is Ahrefs Domain Rating (DR). It measures the strength of a website's backlink profile on a scale from 0 to 100.

For SEO professionals, marketers, and website owners, checking DR helps determine whether a domain has enough authority to rank in search engines or provide valuable backlinks.

However, manually checking domain rating for hundreds of websites can be slow and inefficient.
